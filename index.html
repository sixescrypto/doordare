How It Works

You have two options, you can either "DO" or "DARE".


Darers set dares for the Doers to do on livestream. Darers can also vote on the dares they want to see the most, along with the reward amount.


Doers do the dares the Darers have set on livestream to claim the reward.


- Rewards are paid from the creator fees.
